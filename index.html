What is Gazette?

A Gazette is an official government publication used to notify the public about important legal and personal changes such as name or gender updates.

Prerequisites

  • 18+ years of age
  • GID/GD letter from doctor
  • Email & phone number
  • ID proof
  • Address proof

Modes of Application

1. In Person

Faster, reliable, requires travel to Delhi

2. Via Post

No travel, slower, errors harder to fix

3. Agents

Convenient but risky and expensive
